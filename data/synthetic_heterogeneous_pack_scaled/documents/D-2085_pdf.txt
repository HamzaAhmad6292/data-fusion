This Agreement (“Agreement”) is made as of [DATE] between Telecommunications International (“Client”) and [DESIGNATED LEGAL COUNSEL NAME], hereinafter referred to as “Counsel,” concerning the Client’s request to initiate a regulatory inquiry (“Inquiry”) regarding [Briefly Describe the Inquiry – e.g., “the potential impact of recent network upgrades on local consumer protection standards”].
