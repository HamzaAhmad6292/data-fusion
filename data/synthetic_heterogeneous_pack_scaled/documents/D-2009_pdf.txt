**CONTRACT OF CONFIDENTIALITY & INFORMATION EXCHANGE - REGULATORY INQUIRY**

**Date:** October 26, 2023

**Between:** Telecommunications Holdings Corp (hereinafter referred to as "TC Holdings") and [Client Name/Company Name], hereinafter referred to as “Client”

**WHEREAS,** TC Holdings is engaged in a regulatory inquiry concerning compliance with applicable telecommunications regulations, specifically [Specify the relevant regulation(s) - e.g., the FCC’s Net Neutrality Rules, or a state-specific regulation]; and

**WHEREAS,** Client wishes to provide TC Holdings with confidential information and data pertaining to its operations, network infrastructure, and strategic plans to facilitate this inquiry; and

**NOW, THEREFORE,** in consideration of the mutual agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1.  **Confidentiality Clause:** Client agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all information provided to TC Holdings, including, but not limited to, financial data, technical specifications, operational details, and strategic plans. This confidentiality obligation continues for a period of [Specify Duration – e.g., 6 months] from the date of this Agreement.  Any disclosure of this information outside of TC Holdings’ internal use, without Client’s prior written consent, will be considered a breach of this Agreement.

2.  **Information Exchange:** Client agrees to provide TC Holdings with all data and information requested in connection with the regulatory inquiry, provided that such information is released in a reasonably accessible format (e.g., electronically) and subject to TC Holdings’ reasonable interpretation. TC Holdings will retain ownership of the data provided, but will share the aggregated and anonymized information with relevant regulatory agencies upon request, as permitted by law.

3.  **Data Security & Usage:** TC Holdings will utilize commercially reasonable measures to safeguard the confidentiality and integrity of the information provided, ensuring it is not used for any purpose other than the fulfillment of the regulatory inquiry. TC Holdings will adhere to all applicable data privacy laws and regulations during its use of this information.

4.  **Termination:** Either party may terminate this Agreement with written notice to the other party.  Termination shall be for any reason, including, but not limited to, the completion of the regulatory inquiry.

5.  **Governing Law & Dispute Resolution:** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [State/Jurisdiction]. Any disputes arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in [City, State] under the rules of [Arbitration Organization - e.g., the American Arbitration Association].
