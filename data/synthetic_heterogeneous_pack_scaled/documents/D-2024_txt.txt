**Agreement for Regulatory Inquiry Regarding Healthcare Solutions Inc.**

This Agreement (“Agreement”) is made and entered into as of [Date] by and between Healthcare Solutions Inc. (“Client”) and [Name of Regulatory Agency/Consultant – e.g., the Department of Health and Human Services], (“Regulator”).  This Agreement pertains to a request for a regulatory inquiry by the Regulator concerning Healthcare Solutions Inc.’s compliance with [Specify relevant regulations – e.g., HIPAA, Stark Law, Anti-Kickback Statute]. The Client acknowledges that the Regulator intends to investigate potential violations or non-compliance with these regulations and seeks to cooperate fully with the investigation.

In consideration for the Regulator’s investigation, Healthcare Solutions Inc. agrees to provide the Regulator with full and timely access to all relevant information, documentation, and data pertaining to its operations, practices, and processes. Specifically, the Client agrees to cooperate fully with the Regulator’s inquiries, provide written responses to all requests for information, and maintain a readily available, updated record of all relevant communications. The Client also agrees to waive any and all claims against the Regulator related to the investigation, acknowledging that the Regulator's actions are conducted in the best interests of protecting public health and safety.  This agreement will be governed by the laws of [State/Jurisdiction].

Furthermore, Healthcare Solutions Inc. acknowledges that the investigation may involve questions and inquiries requiring the Client to provide explanations or address specific concerns. The Client understands that the Regulator may request clarification or additional information to facilitate its review of the matter.  The Client agrees to cooperate fully with the Regulator’s requests for information, provided such requests are reasonable, fair, and necessary for the completion of the investigation, and are in accordance with all applicable laws and regulations.

This Agreement is intended to establish a cooperative relationship between Healthcare Solutions Inc. and the Regulator.  Both parties agree to continue their relationship during the course of the investigation.  This Agreement shall be binding and enforceable in accordance with the laws of [State/Jurisdiction].  Both parties have the right to seek legal counsel regarding this agreement.
