**Agreed Upon:** Retail Holdings Corp. (hereinafter “Retail”) and [Client Name], hereinafter referred to as “Client,”

**Date:** October 26, 2023

This agreement, made and entered into as of this date, constitutes a mutual understanding and sets forth the terms and conditions under which Retail agrees to cooperate with [Client Name] regarding a regulatory inquiry initiated by [Regulatory Agency Name] (“Regulatory”). This inquiry pertains to Retail’s compliance with [Specific Regulation - e.g., state consumer protection laws, data privacy regulations].  Retail recognizes the Regulatory’s legitimate interest in ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations and respectfully requests Client’s cooperation in facilitating this process.  The purpose of this agreement is to outline the scope of Client's cooperation, ensure clear communication, and establish a framework for the exchange of information and potential documentation as the Regulatory inquiry progresses.


**Scope of Cooperation:** Client agrees to provide all relevant information, documentation, and data to the Regulatory as requested by the Regulatory. This includes, but is not limited to, providing records of transactions, customer data, marketing materials, and any internal policies related to the subject matter of the inquiry. Client will maintain open and transparent communication with the Regulatory, responding to requests promptly and accurately. Client understands that the Regulatory reserves the right to seek clarification and may require additional information to fully assess compliance.  Specifically, Client will cooperate with the Regulatory's investigation by providing access to all relevant systems and records as outlined in their inquiry’s specific requirements.


**Confidentiality & Data Protection:**  Both parties acknowledge and agree to maintain the confidentiality of all information shared during this process. This includes, but is not limited to, proprietary business data, customer information, and internal operational details.  Client agrees to take all reasonable precautions to protect this information and will promptly notify the Regulatory of any suspected breaches of confidentiality. The agreement also includes a clause safeguarding client’s data privacy, ensuring compliance with all applicable data protection laws and regulations.  Both parties agree that any personal information shared will be used solely for the purpose of the Regulatory inquiry.


**Governing Law & Dispute Resolution:** This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [State/Jurisdiction].  Any disputes arising out of or relating to this agreement shall be resolved through [Mediation/Arbitration - specify method].  Client’s failure to cooperate with the Regulatory may result in a termination of this agreement.
